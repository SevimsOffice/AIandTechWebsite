## 3. LinkedIn Strategy Notes

### Platform Rules to Always Follow
- **Never put external links in the post body** — LinkedIn deprioritizes posts with outbound links. Always put the link in the **first comment**.
- **Always end the post with a CTA line** that points to the first comment: "Link in the comments 👇" or "Yorumda link var."
- **Questions increase comments** — end Pain posts with a genuine question to drive discussion.
- **Save-worthy content** = lists, frameworks, step-by-step breakdowns. Saves boost reach more than likes.

### Lead Capture Flow
```
LinkedIn Post (value/pain/story)
    ↓
First comment with link to landing page
```

## 6. What NOT to Do (Brand Guidelines)

- Do not write posts that feel like ads — lead with value, not product
- Do not put links in post body (LinkedIn penalizes this)
- Do not promise results you can't back up with specifics
- Do not use more than 1-2 emojis per post
- Do not write walls of text — use line breaks after every 1-2 sentences
- Do not use hashtags in the first 3 lines (they reduce reach in LinkedIn's current algorithm)
- Do not write in both Turkish and English in the same post — pick one per post
